# 01 · Brand Essence

## A calm presence in your household.

---

### Mission

Too many people spend their mental energy just keeping up. Olivia is a calm, trustworthy AI companion that handles the overhead of daily life, so every person can feel in control without the constant effort of being in control.

### Positioning

Olivia is not a productivity tool. It is not another app to manage. Olivia is a steady, reliable presence — like a trusted member of the household who absorbs complexity so you don't have to carry it all. It meets people where they are, keeps track of what matters, and speaks up only when it helps.

### Primary Audience

People who feel overwhelmed by the mental load of daily life — particularly neurodiverse individuals, including those with ADHD, who need support managing the many moving parts of running a household and keeping their lives organized. Olivia is designed for anyone who wants to feel in control without the exhausting effort of maintaining that control.

### Brand Personality

Calm · Trustworthy · Steady · Warm · Capable · Unobtrusive
