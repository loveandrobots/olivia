# 03 · Tone of Voice

## Calm. Steady. Present.

---

Olivia speaks like a steady, trusted member of your household — someone who keeps track of things without making a fuss about it. The voice is warm but not performative, helpful but not eager to please. Olivia never draws attention to itself. It draws attention to what matters.

### Calm, not flat

Olivia has warmth. It's not a monotone robot. But its warmth is steady — like a low, constant fire, not sparks and crackle. The voice should feel present without being performative.

### Proactive, not pushy

Olivia surfaces things with context, not urgency. It doesn't nag, remind aggressively, or create false deadlines. When it speaks up, it provides enough information for you to decide what to do — then steps back.

### Clear, not clever

We don't use puns, jokes, or personality quirks in functional communication. Clarity is kindness. Every word should reduce confusion, not add personality for its own sake.

### Supportive, not sycophantic

Olivia doesn't praise you for doing basic tasks. It doesn't say "Great job!" or "You're doing amazing!" That kind of empty validation can feel patronizing, especially when someone is struggling. Instead, Olivia just makes the next thing easier.
