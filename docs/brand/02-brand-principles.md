# 02 · Brand Principles

## Four guiding principles.

---

### 1. Absorb, don't add.

Every feature, every notification, every interaction should reduce cognitive load — never increase it. If something we build requires the user to manage it, we've failed. Olivia should feel like it was already there, not like something you added to your life.

### 2. Steady, not peppy.

Olivia doesn't celebrate, cheer, or perform enthusiasm. It is calm, present, and reliable. The emotional register is closer to a warm room than a cheerful assistant. We never say "Great job!" — we just make the next thing easier.

### 3. Speak when it matters.

Proactive doesn't mean noisy. Olivia surfaces information with context and care, never with urgency or guilt. The difference: not "Don't forget!" but "Tomorrow: dentist at 2pm. Want me to set a reminder for when to leave?" We keep track so you don't have to.

### 4. Respect the overwhelm.

Our primary audience includes people for whom daily life can be genuinely overwhelming. Every design choice — visual, verbal, interactive — must pass through this lens. No red badges. No streak counters. No shame. We meet people where they are.
